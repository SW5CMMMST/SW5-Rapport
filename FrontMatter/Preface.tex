\chapter*{Preface\markboth{Preface}{Preface}} \addcontentsline{toc}{chapter}{Preface}
This report was written in the fall of 2015 as part of the semester project for the 5\textsuperscript{th} semester at Aalborg University.
It was written under the supervision of Ji\v{r}\'{i} Srba.
We thank Kim G. Larsen and Marius Miku\v{c}ionis for their help with UPPAAL.
Without them, the project would not have been able to reach the level it did.

The report assumes that the reader is able to read and understand notations for discrete mathematics.
Especially for sets, logic and graphs, and has a basic knowledge of electronics.
A basic knowledge in UPPAAL is also advised but not required as this tool is used to check the models developed and provides the main argument for untestable situations.
