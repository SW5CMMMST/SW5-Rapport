			Wireless techonology has advanced a lot in the last decade making the internet of things a real thing. 
			
			This project aims to give a reasonable way for smaller embedded devices to wirelessly connect to the internet of things without compromising the advantages of embedded systems.
			These being the small energy usage, cost, and real-time aspects.
			
			Using radio frequency to communicate over short distances is determined as the best solution and a protocol is developed.
